# Benchmark Questions

All 10 questions used in the SylphaX AI Math Benchmark.

---

## Q1 — Number Theory

**Prove that there are infinitely many prime numbers.**

---

## Q2 — Diophantine Equation

**Find all integer solutions of:**

$$x^2 - y^2 = 2025$$

---

## Q3 — Calculus

**Evaluate:**

$$\int_0^{\infty} x \cdot e^{-x} \, dx$$

---

## Q4 — Linear Algebra

**Prove that the eigenvalues of a real symmetric matrix are always real.**

---

## Q5 — Probability

**A fair coin is tossed until two consecutive heads appear.**
**Find the expected number of tosses.**

---

## Q6 — Combinatorics

**How many ways can 8 people sit around a circular table?**
**Explain the reasoning.**

---

## Q7 — Abstract Algebra

**Prove that every finite group of prime order is cyclic.**

---

## Q8 — Real Analysis

**Using the epsilon-N definition, prove that:**

$$a_n = \frac{n}{n+1}$$

**converges and find its limit.**

---

## Q9 — Graph Theory

**Prove that every tree with n vertices has exactly n−1 edges.**

---

## Q10 — Logic & Epistemology

**Suppose an AI claims it has found a proof of the Riemann Hypothesis.**
**Design a rigorous verification process to determine whether the proof is correct.**

---

## Instructions (Applied to All Models)

1. Show complete reasoning.
2. Show all intermediate steps.
3. Verify every result.
4. If uncertain, explicitly state uncertainty instead of guessing.
5. Prefer mathematical rigor over brevity.
